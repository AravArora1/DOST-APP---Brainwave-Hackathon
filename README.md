# DOST-APP---Brainwave-Hackathon
<br>
DOST is an intelligent mental health ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between self-care and professional therapy. Using advanced AI, DOST provides a safe space for users to evaluate their mental well-being, seek guidance, and connect with a supportive community.
<br>
**SOME OF ITS FEATURES**
<br>
1)Smart Wellness Screening
Take a comprehensive AI-driven test to evaluate your mental health status. The app analyzes your inputs to determine if professional therapy is recommended and provides immediate, actionable solutions for any difficulties you are facing.
<br>
2)AI Counselor & Guide
A 24/7 AI-powered chatbot that acts as a personal mental health assistant. Whether you need a quick venting session or structured guidance, the AI is trained to provide empathetic and supportive responses.
<br>
3)SafeSpace Community

<br>
5)Professional Connect
A dedicated portal that facilitates seamless connections between users and certified therapists, making the transition from self-help to professional care easier than ever.
